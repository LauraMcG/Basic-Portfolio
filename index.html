About Me

Hello! I'm an artist. My installations and paintings embody protective forms of withdrawal and non-participation. I utilize physical and chemical properties of paint and phenomenological effects of light to explore the boundaries between our bodies and the environments we inhabit.

Though my formal training has so far centered around painting, I'm now looking to expand my practice from the plane of the canvas to the plane of the screen.
